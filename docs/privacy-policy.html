Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

No data collection

Vibeliner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app has no user accounts, no sign-in, and no registration.

Local storage only

All screenshots and annotations are stored locally on your device in a folder you choose during setup. Vibeliner never reads files outside of your chosen captures folder and the app's own preferences.

No network access

Vibeliner makes no network connections of any kind. It includes no analytics, tracking, telemetry, or crash reporting. No data ever leaves your device.

No cookies or web tracking

Vibeliner does not use cookies or any form of persistent web tracking.

No third-party services

Vibeliner includes no third-party SDKs, frameworks, or services. The app is built entirely with Apple system frameworks.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at privacy@vibeliner.com.
